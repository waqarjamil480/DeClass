DDL, DML 

DDL is used to define, modify, or remove the structure (schema) of database objects 
(like tables, indexes, and views).It Provide the structure to store the data rather than using the stored data for it self
 
CREATE, ALTER, DROP, TRUNCATE, and RENAME


DML is used to manipulate, add, retrieve, or update the data stored inside the database objects. 
If DDL is the architect, DML is the builder moving furniture in and out of the rooms.
